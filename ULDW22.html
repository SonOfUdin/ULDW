ULDW22



ULDW is an annual workshop organised by the Urhobo Studies Association (USA), Delta State University, Abraka, through supports from spirited individuals and organisarions. The workshop aims to bring together Urhobo language teachers, enthusiasts and activists from across Delta Central and beyond to network and discuss on how to develop the Urhobo language for use in education (either as a medium of instruction or as a subject of study). Thus, the goals of the workshop includes:

  1. To promote discussions on revitalisation, growth and development of the Urhobo language, and language inclusion in all spheres of communication.
  2. To create a movement for cooperation with the aim of identifying strategies to accelerate the teaching and learning of Urhobo.
  3. To enhance Urhobo language teaching, learning, acquisition, and research.

#ULDW22 took place on Wed 24th - Thurs. 25th August, 2022 at DESOPADEC Hall, Delta State University Abraka both as in-person and online event. The workshop sought to address the twin issues of Urhobo writing system and scheme of work for teaching the Urhobo language in basic schools.


Prof. R. O. Aziza (FNAL)
Chair, Urhobo Studies Association
